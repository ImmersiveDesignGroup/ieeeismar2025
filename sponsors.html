Sponsoring ISMAR

You are invited to support the ISMAR 2025 conference. This is an ideal way to demonstrate your organization’s interest in and commitment to the field of mixed and augmented reality, and to publicize this support to many leaders and students in the field. Corporate support is used to permit a greater number of students to attend ISMAR at reduced fees without artificially increasing the general registration, to support our diversity initiatives such as the Women’s Lunch and travel grants, and to allow student volunteers to receive complimentary registration. Corporate support can also enable recruitment at the conference during coffee breaks and the demo session, and during this year’s optional sponsor campus tour. ISMAR has historically been sponsored by some of the largest firms in the technology sphere, enabling our community to grow and flourish.

This year, since many of our sponsors have campuses located in the conference city, we will be creating a map of these campuses for ISMAR attendees to visit on Thursday during the day, guided by student volunteers. We see this as an opportunity for sponsors to show off demos and spaces that would not be possible in the confines of the demo session, and of course for recruitment. As sponsors indicate interest, we will finalize the logistics and timing of this activity.

The ISMAR 2025 conference has four corporate support levels: Platinum, Gold, Silver, and Bronze. Your support is critical to the conference, and the conference committee works to design appropriate thank-you gestures. We list the benefits and costs of the support levels below; sponsors are under no obligation to take all benefits.

Platinum Sponsors

Contribution of $20,000 or more

  • Free registrations for 12 people from your company to attend the conference
  • Most prominent placement of company logo on the conference webpage
  • Most prominent placement of company logo on ISMAR’s branded conference slides, shown between sessions and at the beginning of plenaries
  • A table for recruitment during a coffee break in which you are the sole sponsor with a table
  • A table for recruitment during the demo/student innovation contest evening session
  • First choice in destination order in the sponsor campus tour.

Gold Sponsors

Contribution of $15,000 or more

  • Free registrations for 8 people from your company to attend the conference
  • Prominent placement of company logo on the conference webpage
  • Prominent placement of company logo on ISMAR’s branded conference slides, shown between sessions and at the beginning of plenaries
  • A table for recruitment during a coffee break
  • A table for recruitment during the demo/student innovation contest evening session
  • Second choice in destination order in the sponsor campus tour.

Silver Sponsors

Contribution of $10,000 or more

  • Free registrations for 5 people from your company to attend the conference.
  • Placement of company logo on the conference webpage
  • Placement of company logo on ISMAR’s branded conference slides, shown between sessions and at the beginning of plenaries
  • A table for recruitment during the demo/student innovation contest evening session
  • Third choice in destination order in the sponsor campus tour.

Bronze Sponsors

Contribution of $5,000 or more

  • Free registrations for 2 people from your company to attend the conference
  • Placement of company logo on the conference webpage
  • Placement of company logo on ISMAR’s branded conference slides, shown between sessions and at the beginning of plenaries
  • A table for recruitment during the demo/student innovation contest evening session
  • Your office included in the sponsor campus tour.
